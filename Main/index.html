About Us

Welcome to Ohio's loudest backyard since 1968. Blossem was created to give musicains a place to play during the summer. In 2003, Blossom underwent a $17 million renovation to enhance everything from sound system to stage to parking lots and landscaping. Now Blossom is widely used for popular music events from concerts to music festivals.
